About the Project

In this project, myself and my amazing team of volunteers built ADA-approved garden boxes. The purpose of these boxes is for wheelchair-bound students at Granada High School to be able to access the boxes without needing to get out of their chairs.

About the Volunteers and Donors

The volunteers and donors are the real heroes of this project. Without them none of this would've been done. I asked for donations via email and letters, with a goal of raising $500. With the donors' outstanding help, we were able to raise almost $1,200! The volunteers worked tirelessly for 2 days, clearing huge piles of dirt and assembling the boxes with great efficiency.
